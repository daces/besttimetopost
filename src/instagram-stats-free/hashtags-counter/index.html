Instagram can be highly effective, if you use it smart. That's where Instagram hashtags work so well.

Hashtags help you organise and rank for appropriate content on Instagram. They make it simple for your target audience to find you and engage with you. One Instagram study showed that adding at least one hashtag to all of your Instagram posts will, on average, generate over 12% more engagement.

Expand Your Reach

The great things about hashtags is their capability to get to a broad range of people that may follow you on Instagram.

How Do Instagram Hashtags Work?

Example: Let's say a user searches for the term 'cooking'. Instagram will show all the latest posts that contain the 'cooking' term. By using #cooking as a hashtag on your posts, you are communicating with Instagram to put forward your post each time someone lookups that specific term.

Add hashtags to all of your posts with related keywords. Use words and terms you think your users will be searching for. Always use the # symbol before the word, keep it as short as possible and use letters and numbers.
